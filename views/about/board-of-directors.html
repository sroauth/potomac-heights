How do I meet with the Board of Directors?

The Board of Directors meet on the 1st and 3rd Thursday of each month. If you would like to meet with the Board of Directors, please contact the Housing Office at (301) 743-3031 or info@potomacheights.org to schedule an appointment.

Staff Members

Potomac Heights has a staff of nine full-time employees who handle the day-to-day operations of the Cooperation. The staff report to the Property Manager, who keeps the Board and membership informed.

Board of Directors

The members of Potomac Heights Cooperative Housing elect a Board of Director's consisting of eight members who make decisions for the membership, to include directing the Property Manager.
