Potomac Heights Cooperative Housing

Potomac Heights Cooperative Housing is a cooperative community established in 1949 located about 20 miles south of Washington, D.C. in Indian Head, MD. Our community is situated on a bluff overlooking the Potomac River a convenient drive to the Indian Head Naval Support Facility, Waldorf, La Plata, and Washington, DC.

Charles County Recycling

As you know, Charles County takes care of our recycling in Potomac Heights. Please keep in mind that all recycling carts must be taken to the street to be picked up. The recycling folks will not pick up the cart from your yard; they are not the same company that we pay to do our trash pick up.

In order to continue receiving recycling service, all carts must be taken to the street for pick up. Carts that are not taken to the street will not be emptied.
